My Story

I graduated as a Telecommunication Student and stepped straight into the field I love. Today I work at AIS (Advanced Info Service Public Company Limited) in Thailand, one of the country's largest mobile operators, where I focus on 4G and 5G network planning and optimization.

My days revolve around cell coverage analysis, antenna configuration, and RF network planning. When I couldn't find tools that fit my team's workflow, I built them myself. That's how the Radio Planning Tools project was born, starting from a simple tilt calculator and growing into a full suite of engineering instruments.

Beyond telecom, I explore Python development, data visualization with QGIS and Power BI, and experiment with Rust and JavaScript. I believe the best tools come from people who actually use them.
